About

Barrister.

Changemaker.

I'm Karen Safo-Barnieh, a Ghanaian-born, UK-based barrister, speaker and human rights advocate working across law, public policy and social change.

My work moves between the courtroom, the policy table and public platforms: representing people in disputes, advising on global development and governance, and speaking about justice, technology and power. I am also the founder of Upheld, a legal technology platform that puts employment, housing and contract rights directly in the hands of those who need them.
